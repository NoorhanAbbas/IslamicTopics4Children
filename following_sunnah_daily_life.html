Sunnah in Sleeping and Waking

Even sleep becomes worship when done according to the Sunnah! The way you sleep and wake up sets the tone for your entire day and night.

ğŸŒ™ Before Sleeping

The Sunnah Routine:
  • 1. Perform Wudu - Sleep in a state of purity

  • 3. Lie on your right side - The Prophetic sleeping position
  • 4. Place right hand under right cheek
  • 5. Face the Qiblah - If possible
  • 6. Say the bedtime adhkar:
  • - Ayatul Kursi (for protection from shaytan)
  • - Last two verses of Surah Al-Baqarah
  • - Surah Al-Ikhlas, Al-Falaq, An-Nas (3 times each, blow on hands, wipe over body)
  • - Surah Al-Mulk (intercedes for its reciter)
  • 7. Make du'a: "Bismika Allahumma amutu wa ahya" - In Your name O Allah I die and I live
  • 8. Long du'a: "O Allah, I have submitted myself to You..."
  • 9. Sleep early - The Prophet ï·º disliked staying up late unnecessarily
â­ "When you go to bed, perform wudu as you would for prayer, then lie down on your right side..." - Prophet ï·º

â˜€ï¸ Upon Waking

  • 2. Wipe your face - Remove traces of sleep
  • 3. Look at the sky and recite - Last verses of Surah Al-Imran (if you can)
  • 4. Use the siwak/miswak - Clean your teeth
  • 5. Wash your hands - Before putting them in water vessel
  • 6. Perform wudu
