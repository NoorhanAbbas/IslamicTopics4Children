"O you who have believed, protect yourselves and your families from a Fire whose fuel is people and stones."

Equal Treatment Among Children

The Prophet Ô∑∫ emphasized treating children equally. Don't favor boys over girls or one child over another in gifts, attention, or love.

"Fear Allah and treat your children [with equal] fairness." - Prophet Muhammad Ô∑∫

Daughters Have Equal Rights

Islam gives daughters the same rights as sons to love, education, care, and respect. The Prophet Ô∑∫ said:

"Whoever has three daughters or three sisters, or two daughters or two sisters, and lives with them in a good manner and has fear of Allah regarding them, then Paradise is for him."

No Favoritism

When giving gifts, make them equal. When showing affection, be balanced. Favoritism creates resentment, jealousy, and psychological harm.

üåü Practical Parenting Tips üåü

Ages 0-7: Be gentle, play with them, show abundant love and affection.

Ages 7-14: Teach them, discipline with wisdom, encourage good habits.

Ages 14+: Be their friend and advisor, respect their growing independence while guiding them.

Always: Make dua for them constantly, be the best role model, and remember that your patience with them is an act of worship!
