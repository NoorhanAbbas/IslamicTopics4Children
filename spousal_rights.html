Financial Support (Nafaqah)

The wife has an absolute right to her mahr, which is hers to keep and use as she wishes. It is a gift from the husband and a requirement of the marriage contract.

ü§µ Rights of the Husband

The wife should obey her husband in matters that are good and permissible (ma'ruf). She is not obligated to obey in anything sinful or harmful.

üè°

The wife is responsible for managing the household affairs, though this is cooperation, not servitude. Creating a peaceful home environment benefits the whole family.

üîê

The wife should safeguard her husband's honor, reputation, and property in his absence. She should not allow anyone into the home without his knowledge/permission.

üíù

Affection & Companionship

The wife should show love, care, and be a good companion to her husband. The marital relationship should be a source of comfort and tranquility.
